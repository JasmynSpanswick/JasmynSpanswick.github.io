WELCOME TO LINDA ROBERTS DANCE COMPANY

Linda Roberts Dance Company was established in 1969 and has been the best achieving dance school ever since.

We are currently National champions in the United Kingdom, as well as European Champions of 2017.

We also currently have a 99% pass rate in all our examinations, as well as 70% receiving distinctions.

We offer dance classes in a huge range of styles, including Ballet, Hip-Hop, Tap, Lyrical, Irish and Greek. In every style we have a competiton team, and in Ballet, Tap, Modern and Jazz we take examinations.

ABOUT US

The Linda Roberts Dance Company was established in 1969 by Linda Roberts herself, subsequently her grandaughter has taken over and is now the company director.

It has always been dedicated to giving students a strong foundation in classical ballet technique and giving them skills in a variety of other dance styles that they need if they pursue a career in the dance world.

With the exception of our Pre-Ballet classes, all ballet classes are based on the syllabi of the Royal Academy of Dance. Tap and Modern classes are based on the syllabi of the Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing. We also offer IDTA classes for Musical Theatre and Street Dance.

The styles of dance which we offer are: Ballet, Modern, Tap, Jazz, Musical Theatre, Lyrical, Contemporary, Street, Hip-Hop/Commercial and Greek. We also offer voice coach lessons.

Every February we hold a showcase, where every class performs a piece. This is not constrained to the competition teams, everyone has a chance to perform!
